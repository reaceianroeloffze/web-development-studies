Booking Form

Welcome! We are very happy to have you! Thank you for wanting to book your stay with us! Please proceed to fill out this form and secure your accomodations! Booking with us is easy! You can book for 1 person, a duo, a group, your family, whoever. A maximum of 8 people can be booked for 1 stay. All fields are required to fill in.

Personal Information

Your personal information will be stored in our database and used when you want to book future trips with us. Should you be a returning visitor, you will not have to fill out this exact form every time. We also require this information so we can forward you your booked ticket and other relevant information.

Booking Details

They will thus be liable for everything during the duration of your stay.
