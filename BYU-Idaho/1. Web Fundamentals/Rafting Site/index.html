Rapid Fire!

Embark on an epic whitewater thrill ride like no other as these raging river rapids throw everything they have at you, subjecting you to full force of the element known as water! You'll be thrilling, chlling and shrilling as you experience and adrenaline rush like no other! Can you handle what the water gods Neptune and Poseidon have to throw your way? Why don't you find out!

Gently Down the Stream

Prefer a more gentle ride? We've got you covered! Take a pleasing cruise down the gentle river and take in the sights, sounds and calm of the water. You'll become entranced by the melody of nature that sings in your ears.

The River Beckons

Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or someone who just enjoys a glide down a quiet river, we have a place for everyone! Book your rafting equipment and the type of adventure you'd like to go on and heed the call of the river to explore her and all she has to offer. She and We will all be glad to have you.
