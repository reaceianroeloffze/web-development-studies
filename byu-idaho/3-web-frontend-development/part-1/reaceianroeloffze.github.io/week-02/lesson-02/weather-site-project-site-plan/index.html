Purpose

My name is Reace Ian Roeloffze, founder of the new weather site "Nature's Moods". Does it frustrate you when you cannot plan events because you cannot get accurate weather updates or are out of date with goings-on in a particular area? Nature's Moods is a site aimed at providing event planners, news readers, bloggers, community service workers and other audiences with the most current and up-to-date weather patterns, local and regional news, optimal sites for events and detailed weather reports so they can constantly stay up to date with nature's moods and thus plan get the most and the best out of their days and times.
