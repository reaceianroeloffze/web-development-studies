Nature's Moods Site Plan

Target Audience for Weather Site Project

Weather enthusiasts, event planners, outdoor enthusiasts, news lovers, travelers, travel agents, families planning a vactation, couples going on a honeymoon... Anyone looking for a place to go with great weather and lots of events (maybe?), this website is for all those looking to get out. We are a community-based site which offers news reports, reviews and even a forum where people can find out more about events, the best local spots to hit up, the type of weather and much more. Weather reports will be available and other resources, like wind chill factor, humidity, and best time of the year for events or vacations.

Personas

Flora Nuñez

Flora Nuñez is a 32-year-old single event planner for children and adolescents. She has been an event planner for 3 years now and is often tasked with browsing suitable locations that are safe and environmentally friendly for children between the anges of 8 and 11 and adolcecents of all ages. Through this site, she will be able to access weather and news reports about designated areas and a community that will be able to provide her with details on specific and prominent locations for hosting child-friendly events.

Auriel Goosen

Auriel Goosen is a married 40-year-old who earns about R250 000.00 (Rand) a year as a park ranger and manager. As such, he is responsible for the safety and well-being off all who enter his jurisdiction. Using this site, he can alert individuals of potential dangers both past, present and even future, and can even use the knowledge gained from this site to train and instructor presnt and future park rangers and managers who can help to make parks around the world a much safer place.

Scenarios

  • Will the information from this site truly help me plan the best events possible for children and teens?
  • Will this site's information help me adequately do my job of keeping people safe?
  • Can I truly trust the community on their words?
  • Will I be able to contribute to the community forum?
  • How accurate and accessible are the weather and news reports and community forums across different device viewports?
